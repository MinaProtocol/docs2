# Online Communities

The Mina community has many online groups where you can connect with other Mina enthusiasts, node operators, zkApp developers, and more. Come join us!

## Discord

The [Mina Protocol Discord](https://discord.com/invite/RDQc43H) is the most popular place where Mina enthusiasts and technical contributors gather. Some popular channels are listed below, but many more channels exist, so explore the server to find one that fits your interest.

[#announcements](https://discord.gg/JRU3PENEB2) — to follow announcements

[#zkapps-general](https://discord.gg/MVfuuEvD4U) — to ask general questions about zkApps, how to use a zkApp, etc.

[#zkapps-developers](https://discord.gg/v3W4YsuBCw) — to meet other developers building zkApps with SnarkyJS

[#mentor-nodes](https://discord.gg/j9GHr7pJrR) — to get help from the community on setting up and running nodes

[#mainnet-block-producer](https://discord.gg/eAD6V4wW7N) — to meet other node operators and block producers

[#grants](https://discord.gg/AcFCvVvfVW) — to discuss grant opportunities
